BlackThumb is a variation of blackjack made to be played on the Thumby including plenty of diverse features:

*CREDITS FOR BATTERY FUNCTION GO TO AyreGuitar AND ALL THOSE THEY CREDITED*

Blackjack gameplay (hit, stand, double)
Betting system with starting $100
Win streak multiplier
All-in (double or nothing)
Difficulty: NORMAL / HARD 
Saves high score and best streak
Menus: title, bets, tips, stats
Battery indicator
Card graphics and animations
Sound effects and win/lose tunes
Toggle view (player/dealer hand)
